What is

Dumore is an investment group and asset manager dedicated to democratizing access to private real estate investments while building a geographically diverse portfolio across a range of CRE asset classes and risk profiles. Our mission is to create opportunities for investors of all sizes to participate in high-quality, well-researched commercial real estate opportunities that generate long-term value.

Currently, Dumore is focused on revitalizing the aging multifamily housing stock in Long Beach, CA, a market poised for growth due to its proximity to major employment hubs and increasing demand for updated, affordable housing. By repositioning these properties through thoughtful renovations and operational efficiencies, Dumore aims to enhance the living experience for tenants while delivering sustainable returns for investors.

Resilience & Vision

Our Story

The concept for Dumore was born during our founder’s battle with bone cancer. While educating himself about the commercial real estate sector, he identified a critical need for greater accessibility to investments in private commercial real estate.

Gaining Insights

Discussions

Gaining insights from founders who have established CRE investment groups is particularly challenging due to the niche nature of the industry. Through our discussions, we can shed light on the experiences and challenges these founders faced while building their firms.
